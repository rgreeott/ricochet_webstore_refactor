Before Submitting, Please Consider

We accept new and used furniture, home decor, clothing, shoes, jewelry and accessories.  We are unlikely to accept pieces that have severe stains, are visibly worn, or in need of major repairs and/or refinishing. However, if the pieces are considered to be highly sought after, we may still consider them even if the condition is not optimal. PLEASE NOTE: We cannot accept mattresses, nursery items or under garments.

Due to COVID-19 Shutdown, we have started doing limited direct buyout offers on furniture and decor. This is when we make a cash offer for your items and pay your before you leave the store. The value WILL BE less, but you DO NOT have to wait the standard 60 day consignment cycle to see if an item sells. If you are interested in this, please let us know and we will do our best to make things align for both parties.

PLEASE NOTE: If you are a first time submitter or store customer considering consignment, you will be required to process a consignor agreement either at the time of your scheduled appointment or by clicking the button below.
