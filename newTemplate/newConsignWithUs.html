Before Submitting, Please Consider

We are accepting new and used furniture, home decor, clothing, shoes, jewelry and accessories.  We are unlikely to accept pieces that have severe stains, are visibly worn, or in need of major repairs and/or refinishing. However, if the pieces are considered to be highly sought after, we may still consider them even if the condition is not optimal.

NOTE: We cannot accept mattresses, nursery items or under garments.
